About Elige

Jonathan "EliGE" Jablonowski is one of North America's most accomplished CS:GO players, known for his exceptional rifling skills and consistent high-level performance. Born in 1997, he began his professional career in 2014 and quickly became known for his incredibly precise aim and game sense. His journey to the top of competitive Counter-Strike started with Team Liquid in 2015, where he would become a cornerstone of North American CS:GO for years to come.

EliGE's reputation as one of the world's best riflers comes from his unique combination of mechanical skill and intelligent positioning. His mastery of spray control, particularly with the AK-47 and M4, sets him apart from other players. What makes him exceptional is his ability to maintain extremely high accuracy while moving and engaging multiple opponents, a skill that has led many analysts to consider him North America's greatest rifler of all time. His success isn't just based on raw aim - his deep understanding of game mechanics and ability to adapt to different metas has kept him at the top of the game for nearly a decade.

Throughout his career, EliGE has achieved numerous notable accomplishments. With Team Liquid, he was instrumental in their historic run during 2019, when they won the Intel Grand Slam Season 2 in a record-breaking 63 days, claiming victories at multiple major tournaments including ESL One Cologne and IEM Chicago. He's been named HLTV's Top 20 Players list multiple times, reflecting his consistent excellence at the highest level of competition. As of 2024, EliGE continues to compete at the highest level, now playing for Cloud9 after his long tenure with Team Liquid came to an end in late 2023. Despite changes in the competitive landscape and his team affiliation, he remains one of Counter-Strike's most respected players, known for his professionalism, work ethic, and continued excellence in the game.
